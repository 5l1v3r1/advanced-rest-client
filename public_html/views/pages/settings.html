Analytics

This app is using Google Analytics Platform to collect usage data.

None of your personal or your work's data is collected by the app. There's no possibility to recognize a person from collected data.

You can disable sending any statistical data. However, this data help determine how the app is used by all the users, what is screen size, chrome version etc. It will help to develop new features and help create more usable application for you.

History synchronization

The app will synchronize your Requests history between your computers. This data are only avaliable for you.

You need to be logged in to your Google Account in order to this feature work.

Clear history

Remove history data. Note that it will be removed from all your devices.
